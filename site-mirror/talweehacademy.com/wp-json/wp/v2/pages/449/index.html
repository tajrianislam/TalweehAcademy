What is Talweeh Academy?<\/strong><\/p>

Talweeh Academy is an institution dedicated to reviving Islamic academia in the West. Our mission is to help seekers of knowledge engage deeply with the rich legacy of Islamic scholarship. We achieve this by offering a carefully designed syllabus featuring robust, content-rich courses that delve into essential concepts within every Islamic science.\u00a0<\/p>

Our courses are taught by highly qualified instructors who have years of training and experience in their respective fields. These instructors bring thorough research and expertise, ensuring that each student receives an authentic and comprehensive education.<\/span>Our comprehensive curriculum encompasses a wide range of subjects, including: <\/span><\/p>

  1. Na\u1e25w<\/b> (grammar)<\/span><\/li>
  2. \u1e62arf<\/b> (morphology)<\/span><\/li>
  3. Bal\u0101ghah<\/b> (rhetoric)<\/span><\/li>
  4. Mantiq<\/b> (logic)<\/span><\/li>
  5. Tafs\u012br <\/b>(exegesis)<\/li>
  6. <\/b>U\u1e63\u016bl al-\u1e24ad\u012bth<\/b> (foundations of \u1e25ad\u012bth)<\/span><\/li>
  7. U\u1e63\u016bl al-Fiqh<\/b> (foundations of jurisprudence)<\/span><\/li>
  8. Fiqh <\/b>(Islamic jurisprudence)<\/span><\/li>
