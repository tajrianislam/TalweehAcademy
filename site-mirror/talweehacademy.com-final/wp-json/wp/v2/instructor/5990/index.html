Sheikh Omer Khurshid was raised in Toronto, Canada, where he began his spiritual journey by memorizing the Quran at the age of ten. This early commitment to the sacred text laid the foundation for his later academic pursuits. Motivated by a desire to deepen his knowledge, he enrolled in the \u2018\u0100limiyyah program at Jame Abu Bakr Siddique in Toronto, Canada. There, he engaged in a comprehensive study of Islamic sciences spanning 7 years. Upon completing the \u2018\u0100limiyyah program, the Sheikh embarked on a journey to Madinah, where he dedicated himself to the profound study of \u2018Ul\u016bm al-\u1e25ad\u012bth at the Islamic University of Madinah. After spending 6 years in the blessed city of Madinah, the Sheikh graduated from the Faculty of \u1e24ad\u012bth, immersing himself in the intricacies of this vital discipline.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Studies in Madinah<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

During his time in Madinah, the Sheikh had the unique privilege of studying under the guidance of the Sh\u0101fiee Mu\u1e25aqqiq, Sheikh Safw\u0101n D\u0101wood\u012b, upon whom he studied multiple texts in Nahw, Sh\u0101fiee Fiqh, U\u1e63\u016bl, Aqeedah, \u1e24ad\u012bth and other sciences. He also undertook extensive studies of texts of \u1e24ad\u012bth multiple times under the guidance of Sheikh H\u0101mid Al-Bukh\u0101r\u012b and others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Comprehensive Study<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

The Sheikh\u2019s academic journey encompasses a thorough examination of the texts from all four schools of thought, engaging deeply with their respective scholars. He has also explored both methodologies in U\u1e63\u016bl al-fiqh\u2014\u1e6car\u012bqah al-fuqah\u0101\u02be and \u1e6car\u012bqah al-mutakallim\u012bn\u2014and delved into various branches of U\u1e63\u016bl al-d\u012bn. This diverse and comprehensive background reflects his commitment to understanding the rich tapestry of Islamic jurisprudence and theology.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Teaching Experience<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

For the past several years, the Sheikh has dedicated himself to teaching these vital sciences, sharing his knowledge and insights with students eager to learn. His teaching not only imparts academic knowledge but also inspires a love for the pursuit of knowledge and a deeper understanding of the Islamic faith. Through his extensive studies and teaching, the Sheikh continues to contribute significantly to the scholarship of Islamic sciences, nurturing the next generation of learners and scholars in the process.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Expanded scholarship<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

In addition to his focus on \u1e25ad\u012bth, the Sheikh broadened his scholarly pursuits to include the sciences of Na\u1e25w, Fiqh, U\u1e63\u016bl al-fiqh, U\u1e63\u016bl al-d\u012bn, and Qir\u0101’\u0101t and he received Ij\u0101zah from the distinguished Q\u0101r\u012b Bash\u012br Ahmad Siddique. He has also received traditional Ij\u0101zah from over 100 scholars worldwide including: Shaykh \u02bfAbd ar-Ra\u1e25m\u0101n Katt\u0101n\u012b (Morocco), Shaykh Fa\u1e0dl ar-Ra\u1e25\u012bm al-Amritsar\u012b al-Ashraf\u012b (Pakistan), Shaykh Wal\u012b al-\u1e24aqq a\u1e63-\u1e62idd\u012bq\u012b (Afghanistan), Shaykh Mus\u0101\u02bfid Bash\u012br as-Sidr\u0101t\u012b (Sudan), Shaykh Mu\u1e25ammad \u1e0c\u0101min \u02bfAl\u012b al-Hind\u012b (India), Shaykh \u02bf\u0100qil al-Ma\u1e93\u0101har\u012b (India), Shaykh \u1e24ab\u012bb Ja\u02bffar as-Saqq\u0101f (Yemen) and many others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
