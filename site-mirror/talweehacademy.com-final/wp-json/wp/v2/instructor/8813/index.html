Shaykh Farhan Ingar began his journey in the pursuit of sacred knowledge at a young age in Toronto, Canada. He memorized the Qur\u2019an through the part-time Hifz program at the Islamic Foundation of Toronto, where he developed a deep connection with the Book of Allah.
Motivated to further his studies, Shaykh Farhan enrolled in the rigorous seven-year \u02bf\u0100limiyyah program at J\u0101mi\u02bfah Isl\u0101miyyah Ab\u016b Bakr in Toronto, Canada. During this period, he engaged in an in-depth study of the classical Islamic sciences, delving deeply into the Arabic language\u2014including Na\u1e25w, \u1e62arf, and Bal\u0101ghah\u2014as well as Islamic jurisprudence (Fiqh), Qur\u2019\u0101nic exegesis (Tafs\u012br), and the major books of \u1e24ad\u012bth, among many other disciplines within the traditional curriculum. Alongside his formal studies, he also studied privately with scholars locally and internationally, further strengthening his grounding in the Islamic sciences through close scholarly mentorship. He completed the \u02bf\u0100limiyyah program gaining a strong grounding in the classical scholarly tradition of Islam.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Ift\u0101\u02be Specialization<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Continuing his pursuit of advanced scholarship, Shaykh Farhan is currently enrolled in a specialization program in Ift\u0101\u02be. Through this training, he is further developing his expertise in Islamic legal reasoning, the methodology of issuing legal verdicts (fat\u0101w\u0101), and the application of classical jurisprudence to contemporary issues.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Teaching and Community Service<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Alongside his studies, Shaykh Farhan is actively engaged in teaching and community service. He currently serves full-time as an instructor for Tajw\u012bd at Madrassa Faidul Qur\u02be\u0101n at Masjid D\u0101r al-Sal\u0101m in Toronto, Canada. In this role, he oversees Qur\u2019\u0101n instruction and Tajw\u012bd education, helping Hifdh & \u02bf\u0100limiyyah students perfect their recitation while cultivating a deeper appreciation for the Qur\u2019\u0101n.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Expanded Scholarship<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

In addition to his formal studies, Shaykh Farhan has received multiple ij\u0101z\u0101t (\u02bf\u0101mmah and kh\u0101\u1e63\u1e63ah) in the sciences of \u1e24ad\u012bth and other Islamic disciplines from a number of distinguished scholars. Among those from whom he has been granted ij\u0101zah are Shaykh Ayyub Surti from England, Shaykh Ibrahim Dewla from India, Shaykh Imdad Hasan from Pakistan, Shaykh \u02bfAli Salih al-Azhari from Egypt, and the renowned scholar of Qur\u2019\u0101nic recitation Shaykh Yahya al-Ghawthani from Turkey, among
